Polycom C60 · WebHID SDP proof of concept

Drive the i.MX8MM BootROM serial-download protocol (SDP) from the browser over WebHID — no uuu, no driver install. Load flash.bin into RAM in two stages, exactly like uuu -b spl.

Pick the C60 build output (polycom-uboot/out/c60-kepler_proto1/flash.bin). The page parses the two boot containers (SPL + U-Boot) and shows the load addresses it will use.

1 · BootROM SDP — load & run SPL validated parse

Put the C60 in recovery (both BOOT_MODE switches OFF), plug USB. Click to grant the 1fc9:0134 HID device, then it writes the SPL to OCRAM and jumps. Success = the SPL brings up DDR and prints on the UART console.

2 · SPL SDPV — load U-Boot experimental

After the SPL re-enumerates it appears as a new HID device. Click to grant it, then the U-Boot container is written to DRAM and jumped. Stage-2 addressing is the HW-VERIFY part — watch the console.
